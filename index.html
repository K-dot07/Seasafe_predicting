How SeaSafe Works

Understanding our machine learning approach to maritime safety prediction

Machine Learning Pipeline

1. Data Collection

We analyze historical maritime data including passenger records, weather conditions, and vessel characteristics from multiple sources.

2. Feature Engineering

Our algorithms process ship age, capacity, weather patterns, route difficulty, and seasonal factors to create predictive features.

3. Model Training

Multiple machine learning models are trained including Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and Neural Networks for optimal accuracy.

4. Ensemble Prediction

Results from multiple models are combined with confidence scoring to provide the most reliable safety assessment.
